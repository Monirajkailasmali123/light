  • 2017Light Samvedna has the honor of being First light centre. Light samvedna started its journey in 2017, and has since undergone meteoric rise. From that point of time LiGHT samvedna has played an important role and has had a significant impact on the lives of people.
  • May 2019 Blood Donation Camp - There was a huge response from the students; about 98 students donated their blood. Poster making competition was also held.
  • June 2019 - January 2020 Orphanage Visit Our club regularly visits Bhagwan Baba Balikashram for multiple objectives like tutoring children to solve their problems, organizing creative games and celebrating various occasions. Visits are monthly/weekly.
  • January 2020 Awareness drive - Students from the first year addressed school kids for celebrating a safe and green Diwali. This event was done under the guidance of Santosh Tapkire sir( principal of Z P School).
  • September 2020 RISE campaign - The full form of RISE is Reform and Innovate School Education which is a campaign to make some positive changes to make the teaching and learning process more fruitful. We had a workshop for teachers of primary and pre-primary schools from Aurangabad. The workshop was of 5 days 2-3 hrs per day where trainers from IIT Kharagpur trained teachers.
  • January 2021 CCP campaign -Career Counselling Program (CCP) was launched on 25th Jan with the prime aim to connect students of class 9-12th with college students who have outshined in their respective part and are motivated enough to transfer their experience to the budding youth.
  • April 2021 Saving Wings from Fire - This was a collaborative activity with the Evolve club, GECA. Under this, we worked towards providing shelter for birds in summer times. 26 participants were involved.
  • May 2021 COVI RELIEF campaign - This was a donation drive to help to provide food to the ones in need. We collected donations from people and this was donated to the Annamrita Foundation. We collected a total of Rs 8025 which helped serve 105 meals and was a very satisfying work we did.
  • October 2021 Inter Center Debate Competition - LiGHT Samvedna won this competition among the other centers involved.
  • December 2021 Be A Santa We, at Samvedna, wanted to bring a smile on the face of orphans on Christmas Day. We started a fundraising campaign to raise money for various activities that we were going to undertake.
  • February 2022 Sponsorship program - Under this, we are trying to connect financially weak students who cannot afford to study to organisations or schemes that provide funds or financial assistance. Work is in progress.
