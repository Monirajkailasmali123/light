What is LiGHT Samvedna?

LiGHT Samvedna is a group of students from Government College of Engineering Aurangabad working under the Student-run NGO of IIT Kharagpur called Gopali Youth Welfare Society. LiGHT Samvedna is a center for people who think beyond themselves, who look beyond themselves, and care to support not just the less privileged but all the possible people who need support. So what is our motto? The main motto is, creating leaders who solve social problems with engineering skills and help to build a better future. The base for a happy and prosperous future is a concrete present, which can be built by collectively working towards a healthy society! So let's join our hands together for a bright future.
